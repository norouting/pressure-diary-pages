Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Data stored by the app

The app may store blood pressure readings, pulse values, dates and times, notes, wellbeing status, measurement context, app settings, and subscription-related state required for app functionality.

Where data is stored

Core diary data is stored on your device. If you use Apple system features, some data may be handled by iOS according to your device settings and Apple policies.

Apple Health

If you grant Apple Health access, the app may read and save blood-pressure-related data to help keep your diary complete. You can change or revoke Apple Health access in iOS Settings at any time.

PDF reports

PDF reports are created only when you choose to export them. You decide who receives or sees a report. A report may include blood pressure, pulse, dates, notes, and measurement context.

Sharing with third parties

We do not sell your health data. Data may be shared only when required for features you choose to use, such as system PDF sharing, Apple Health, or App Store subscription processing.

Subscriptions and purchases

Purchases and subscriptions are processed by the App Store. We do not receive your payment card details. You can manage subscriptions through your Apple ID settings.

Diagnostics

Apple may provide developers with aggregated crash and performance information if this is allowed in your device settings.

Deleting data

You can delete individual readings in the app. Deleting the app removes local app data unless the data was also saved in another system service, such as Apple Health.
